How to Convert Images

  1. Select Images: Click on "Upload Images" or "Upload Folder" to upload images from your device. Alternatively, enter an image URL to add images from the web.
  2. Choose Format: By default, the selected format will apply to all images. Uncheck "Use same format for all images" to set individual formats.
  3. Convert Images: Click "Convert All" to start the conversion process.
  4. Download: After conversion, download your images individually or as a ZIP file.
